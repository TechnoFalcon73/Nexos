About Oscar

Hi, I'm Oscar Coleman, a teenage writer, student, and entrepreneur from Sydney, Australia. Science and philosophy are my passion, and I'm committed to shaping a better future inspired by luminaries like Elon Musk.
Recent innovations and discoveries in science mixed with my involvement in science through extracurricular activities and studies have motivated and fueled my desire to think about the technologies and world of the future.
By researching and involving myself in futurism, I have begun to understand that we need to consider our choices in the present to make a future worth living for.
My blog, Nexus, allows me to share my interest in science and the future. Here I aim to highlight crucial issues and inspire collaboration to drive meaningful and lasting change.
