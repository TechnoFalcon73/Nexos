About Oscar

Oscar Coleman is a teenage writer, student and entrepreneur from Sydney, Australia. As a dedicated student of science and philosophy, he is fixed on shaping and working towards a future worth living for.
Inspired by luminaries such as Elon Musk, Oscar deeply believes that to shape a brighter tomorrow, we must first imagine it. With this in mind, he is committed to exploring the challenges that lie ahead, using insights gained to cultivate a more hopeful and fulfilled future.
Nexus serves as a means to bring awareness to our future and what we need to focus on today to bring about a better world tomorrow. Though the road to the future is long and winding, and may have many bumps, Oscar remains undeterred, driven by his deep conviction that collective effort and collaboration can open the doorway to meaningful and lasting change.
At the core of Oscar's philosophy is the belief that universal morality should guide our actions, in line with the teachings of great thinkers like Immanuel Kant. By adhering to the rule "Act only according to that maxim whereby you can at the same time will that it should become a universal law," we can strive towards a world that is grounded in consideration and understanding. To create a future worth living for.
