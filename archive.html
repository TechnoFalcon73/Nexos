Will we grow extinct? Or will we endure? Will we colonise the stars and make Star Trek a reality? At Nexos, we delve into questions like these, questioning the increasingly hazy line between sci-fi and sci-fact. We consider various topics on artifcial intelligence, genetics, space exploration, humans on Earth and emerging technologies, analysing them and what they may lead to. Our mission is to create a completely accessible online discussion for futurism and science, raising questions and considerations about the future of our race, and to bring awareness to the future of humanity and the problems we face in our world. Are we bound for extinction or will we continue to endeavour toward the stars? And if so, what will that do to our society -- to humanity?
